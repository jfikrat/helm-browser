## [​](https://docs.browserbase.com/introduction\#why-browserbase)  Why Browserbase?

Browserbase makes it easy to use fleets of headless browsers with:

- Native compatibility with [Playwright](https://docs.browserbase.com/introduction/playwright), [Puppeteer](https://docs.browserbase.com/introduction/puppeteer), [Selenium](https://docs.browserbase.com/introduction/selenium) and integration with your favorite AI stack, from [crewAI](https://docs.browserbase.com/integrations/crew-ai/introduction) to [Langchain](https://docs.browserbase.com/integrations/langchain/introduction).
- Full observability of Sessions with the [Session Inspector](https://docs.browserbase.com/features/session-inspector).
- [Stealth mode](https://docs.browserbase.com/features/stealth-mode) provides automatic captcha solving and residential proxies.
- Support for advanced features such as [Custom Extensions](https://docs.browserbase.com/features/browser-extensions), [File downloads](https://docs.browserbase.com/features/downloads) and long-running Sessions.
- An extensible platform with APIs to [integrate a live view](https://docs.browserbase.com/features/session-live-view) or [retrieve logs and recordings](https://docs.browserbase.com/reference/api/session-logs).
- Secure, scalable, and reliable infrastructure with [fair and transparent pricing](https://www.browserbase.com/).
